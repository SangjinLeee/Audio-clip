Maroon 5 is an American pop-rock band that has been active since the early 2000s. They have had numerous chart-topping hits throughout their career, including "Maps" and "Sugar." "Maps" was released in 2014 as the lead single from their fifth studio album, "V." The song features an upbeat melody and heartfelt lyrics about the pain of a failed relationship. "Sugar," on the other hand, was released in 2015 and quickly became a fan favorite. The song features a catchy chorus and an infectious beat that has made it a popular choice for weddings and other celebratory events. Both "Maps" and "Sugar" showcase Maroon 5's ability to create memorable and engaging pop songs that have resonated with fans around the world.
